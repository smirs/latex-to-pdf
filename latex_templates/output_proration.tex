	\section{PURPOSE.}
	The purpose of this Addendum is to clarify the proration of rent for a \CuteBox{\the\PreLeaseDuration} days in the time period from the moving-in date of \DateBox{\Commencement} to the start date of the lease on \DateBox{\LeaseStart}. 

	\section{PRORATION CALCULATION.}
	Based on the monthly rent of \SmallBox{\$\Rent}, the prorated rent amount is calculated by multiplying the daily rent by the number of days in the proration period. That means \SmallBox{\$\DailyRent} multiplied by \TinyBox{\the\PreLeaseDuration} days from \DateBox{\Commencement} to \DateBox{\LeaseStart}. The resulting prorated rent amount is \DateBox{\$\DueAmount}. 
	
	Following is the detailed breakdown of the proration calculation:

	\[
	\begin{aligned}
	\text{Daily Rent} 		&	= \frac{\Rent}{30} = \$\DailyRent \\
	\text{Prorated Rent} 	&	= \$\DailyRent \times \num{\the\PreLeaseDuration} = \SmallBox{\$\DueAmount}	
	\end{aligned}
	\]

	\section{PAYMENT TERMS.}
	This prorated rent shall be due on or before \DateBox{\Commencement} and shall be paid in accordance with the payment terms outlined in the original lease agreement.
