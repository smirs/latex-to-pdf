	\section{PURPOSE.}
	The purpose of this Addendum is to outline the proration of rent for the period from \DateBox{\Commencement}, to \DateBox{\LeaseStart}, based on the monthly rent of \SmallBox{\$\Rent}. 

	\section{PRORATION CALCULATION.}
	The prorated rent amount is calculated by multiplying the daily rent by the number of days in the proration period, which is \SmallBox{\$\DailyRent} multiplied by \TinyBox{\the\PreLeaseDuration} days from \DateBox{\Commencement} to \DateBox{\LeaseStart}. The resulting prorated rent amount is \DateBox{\$\DueAmount}.

	\[
	\begin{aligned}
	\text{Daily Rent} &= \frac{\Rent}{30} = \$\DailyRent \\
	\text{Prorated Rent} &= \text{Daily Rent} \times \text{Number of Days in Proration Period} \\
						&= \$\DailyRent \times \the\PreLeaseDuration = \$\DueAmount
	\end{aligned}
	\]

	\section{PAYMENT TERMS.}
	The Tenant agrees to pay the prorated rent amount of \DateBox{\$\DueAmount{}}. This prorated rent shall be due on or before \DateBox{\Commencement} and shall be paid in accordance with the payment terms outlined in the original lease agreement.
